Justice

This project examines justice not as a fixed moral value but as a system shaped by power and perspective. The title references the homophone "just us," questioning structures that claim fairness while protecting only a few. Influenced by MF DOOM's Strange Ways, the Strangeways Prison Riot, and The Shawshank Redemption, metal becomes a central symbol — neutral in itself but transformed through human intervention.

Mutation

Inspired by AKIRA, this collection explores the uncertainty of transitioning into adulthood. The designs reflect emotional and physical transformation through exaggerated volume and evolving forms. Mutation becomes a metaphor for growth, adaptation, and identity in chaotic environments.
